# Art Styles

All available art styles for No Context quote illustrations.

---

## Free Styles

### Watercolor

**ID:** `watercolor`
Soft, flowing paintings with gentle color washes and visible brushstrokes. Uses loose brushstrokes with white paper showing through for a delicate, airy feel.

### Van Gogh / Post-Impressionist

**ID:** `vangogh`
Swirling brushstrokes with vivid colors and emotional intensity. Captures the raw, expressive energy of Vincent van Gogh's iconic technique.

### Monet / Impressionist

**ID:** `monet`
Soft light and dappled colors capturing fleeting atmospheric moments. Recreates Claude Monet's signature impressionist style with dreamy atmospheric effects.

### Warhol / Pop Art

**ID:** `warhol`
Bold, flat colors with graphic outlines inspired by pop culture. Channels Andy Warhol's bright flat colors, bold outlines, and repeated motifs.

### Hokusai / Ukiyo-e

**ID:** `hokusai`
Japanese woodblock prints with flowing lines and flat color areas. Inspired by Hokusai's dynamic compositions with traditional ukiyo-e aesthetics.

### Rockwell / Americana

**ID:** `rockwell`
Warm, detailed illustrations with classic American storytelling charm. Norman Rockwell-style compositions with rich character detail and narrative warmth.

### Pencil Sketch

**ID:** `sketch`
Hand-drawn pencil illustrations with cross-hatching and expressive shading. Features varying line weights and detailed cross-hatching on white paper.

### Stained Glass

**ID:** `stainedglass`
Jewel-toned translucent colors with bold black leading lines. Mimics cathedral stained glass with radiant light filtering through rich colors.

## Premium Styles

### Picasso / Cubism

**ID:** `picasso`
Abstract geometric shapes showing multiple perspectives at once. Cubist compositions with geometric fragmentation, bold outlines, and simultaneous viewpoints.

### Dali / Surrealism

**ID:** `dali`
Dreamlike landscapes with melting forms and impossible physics. Salvador Dali-inspired surrealism with distorted reality and otherworldly scenery.

### Mondrian / De Stijl

**ID:** `mondrian`
Abstract grids of primary colors separated by bold black lines. Piet Mondrian's signature geometric abstraction with primary colors and white space.

### Basquiat / Neo-Expressionism

**ID:** `basquiat`
Raw, energetic street art with bold marks and expressive symbols. Jean-Michel Basquiat's neo-expressionist energy with crowns, bold marks, and street art grit.

### Miyazaki / Studio Ghibli

**ID:** `miyazaki`
Lush, whimsical anime landscapes with a sense of wonder. Studio Ghibli-style illustrations with lush environments and whimsical characters.

### Comic Book

**ID:** `comic`
Bold ink lines, halftone dots, and dynamic comic panel compositions. Vibrant panels with speech bubbles, dynamic angles, and classic comic energy.

### Pixel Art

**ID:** `pixel`
Retro video game aesthetic with clean pixels and limited color palettes. Detailed pixel scenes with nostalgic 8/16-bit era charm.

### K-Pop Demon Hunters

**ID:** `kpop`
Stylish K-pop idol aesthetic fused with dark fantasy demon hunting. Features sleek idol outfits, glowing weapons, neon-lit supernatural battlegrounds, dramatic poses, and manhwa-inspired linework.

### Fortnite

**ID:** `fortnite`
Vibrant 3D cartoon style with exaggerated proportions and bold outlines. Cel-shaded characters with saturated colors, playful action poses, and colorful stylized environments.

### Archer

**ID:** `archer`
Clean cel-shaded spy-thriller style with muted, sophisticated colors. Uniform-weight black outlines, flat fills, mid-century modern settings, and cinematic atmosphere. Zero gradients or texture.

### South Park

**ID:** `southpark`
Simple flat 2D animation with geometric shapes and minimal aesthetic. Clean digital vector animation with flat solid colors, no gradients, thin black outlines, and geometric simplicity.

### Futurama

**ID:** `futurama`
Colorful retro-futuristic cartoon style with Groening-style characters. Large bulging round eyes, overbites, bulbous noses, bold saturated flat fills, and retro-futuristic settings.

### The Simpsons

**ID:** `simpsons`
Bright, cheerful flat animation with yellow skin and overbites. Thick uniform black outlines, huge circular white eyes, four-fingered hands, flat solid fills, and pastel suburban settings.

### Fallout

**ID:** `fallout`
Retro-futuristic 1950s Americana with atomic age propaganda poster vibes. Warm faded colors (mustard yellow, teal, cream, rust red), exaggerated happy expressions, and Cold War-era graphic design.

## Future Style Ideas

### Oil Painting / Classical

Rich, textured oil painting style with dramatic lighting and classical composition. Think Rembrandt or Vermeer with deep shadows and luminous highlights.

### Art Nouveau

Elegant flowing organic lines, decorative floral motifs, and ornate borders. Inspired by Alphonse Mucha's poster art with rich earthy and jewel tones.

### Banksy / Street Art

Stencil-style street art with political undertones, monochrome with selective color pops, and gritty urban wall textures.

### Vaporwave

Retro 80s/90s digital aesthetic with neon pink and cyan gradients, glitch effects, Roman busts, palm trees, and grid landscapes.

### Claymation

Stop-motion clay animation style with visible finger textures, rounded forms, and tactile warmth. Think Wallace & Gromit or Coraline.

### Art Deco

Geometric luxury with bold symmetry, metallic gold accents, and glamorous 1920s-30s design. Sharp angles, sunburst patterns, and rich jewel tones.

### Impressionist Dot / Pointillism

Tiny distinct dots of color that blend optically from a distance. Inspired by Georges Seurat with vibrant, shimmering compositions.

### Gothic / Dark Fantasy

Dark, moody illustrations with ornate details, dramatic shadows, and medieval-inspired elements. Gargoyles, stone textures, and candlelit atmospheres.

### Lofi / Cozy Anime

Warm, soft anime style with muted pastel tones, gentle lighting, and cozy indoor scenes. Inspired by the lofi hip hop aesthetic.

### Bauhaus

Clean geometric minimalism with primary colors, sans-serif typography influence, and functional design. Bold shapes and structured compositions.

### Kawaii / Chibi

Cute Japanese illustration style with oversized heads, tiny bodies, sparkly eyes, pastel colors, and adorable expressions.

### Noir / Film Noir

High-contrast black and white with dramatic shadows, venetian blind lighting, rain-slicked streets, and moody detective thriller atmosphere.

### Risograph

Textured print aesthetic with misregistered layers, limited color palette (typically 2-3 spot colors), grain texture, and indie zine vibes.

### Woodcut / Linocut

Bold black and white relief print style with strong carved lines, high contrast, and handmade texture. Dramatic and graphic.

### Anime / Shonen

Dynamic action-oriented anime with speed lines, dramatic lighting, intense expressions, and vibrant hair colors. Think Naruto or My Hero Academia energy.

### Vintage Travel Poster

Mid-century travel poster style with flat bold colors, simplified landscapes, elegant typography influence, and aspirational scenic compositions.

### Bob Ross / Happy Little Trees

Soft, soothing landscape paintings with happy little trees, calm water reflections, mountain ranges, and thick palette knife textures.

### Cyberpunk

Neon-drenched dystopian cityscapes with holographic ads, rain, chrome surfaces, and high-tech-low-life atmosphere.

### Children's Book Illustration

Whimsical, friendly illustrations with soft textures, warm colors, and storybook charm. Think Eric Carle or Oliver Jeffers.

### Graffiti / Wildstyle

Bold graffiti lettering and characters with vibrant spray paint colors, drips, tags, and urban wall backgrounds.

### Realistic / Photorealistic

Hyper-realistic digital illustration that mimics photography. Lifelike lighting, accurate textures, natural skin tones, and cinematic depth of field. Scenes look like they could be real photographs.
