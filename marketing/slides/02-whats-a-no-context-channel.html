# no-context

What's a
#no-context
channel?

It's a Slack channel where your team posts the funniest things people say at work — completely out of context.

No setup. No explanation. Just the quote, the person, and the chaos.

Thousands of teams already have one. No Context Bot turns those quotes into art.
